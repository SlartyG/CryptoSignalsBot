What is this bot?

Midas Crypto Signals is a Telegram bot that sends market anomaly alerts for crypto derivatives (Bybit exchange): extreme funding, open interest changes, liquidation spikes, and volume spikes.

These are not "buy/sell" signals and not financial advice. The bot informs you about unusual market activity — trading decisions are yours alone.

How do I get started?

1. Find the bot in Telegram and tap /start.
2. Choose your language (RU / EN / UA).
3. Subscribe to required channels (for Free) and confirm.
4. Accept the Terms and Privacy Policy.
5. Configure signals in Settings.

Is there auto-renewal?

No. Your subscription ends on the stated date. The bot reminds you 7, 3, and 1 day before expiry. Renew manually by purchasing a new period.

Can I get a refund?

Subscriptions are a digital service activated immediately after payment. Refunds are only available if the Operator fails to activate paid access within 72 hours of confirmed payment due to the Operator's fault. Payment errors (wrong network, amount) are reviewed individually — contact support.

Why am I not receiving alerts?

Possible reasons:
- No anomalies — the bot only alerts when thresholds trigger, not continuously.
- Cooldown — the same signal type per pair won't repeat for 30–60 minutes.
- Settings — signal type or pair disabled in Settings.
- Free plan — BTCUSDT only.
- Telegram — ensure the bot isn't blocked and notifications are on.
- Expired subscription (Paid) — renew access.

If the issue persists — @midas_crypto_support.

Where does the data come from?

Bybit public API V5 (funding, OI, tickers, liquidations WebSocket). No exchange API key is required. Data may be delayed — normal for automated services.

What signal types are available?

1. Funding Extreme — extreme funding rate (long/short imbalance).
2. OI + Price — open interest change with price movement.
3. Liquidation Spike — large liquidation burst.
4. Volume Spike — hourly volume well above normal.

Each type can be toggled in Settings.

Is this a profit guarantee?

No. Crypto trading is highly risky. The bot does not guarantee accuracy, timeliness, or profitability. Past data does not predict the future. Consult professionals when needed.

Can I share access with a friend?

No. Access is tied to your Telegram account. Resale, public republication of alerts, and automated scraping are prohibited and may result in a ban.

Bot not responding / payment error

2. Check internet and Telegram status.
3. For payments — ensure Crypto Pay is available in your region and the invoice is paid in time.
